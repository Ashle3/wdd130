Meet Bailey!

Bailey is an 8-year-old female Staffordshire Terrier that has been with us since 2018. She is sweet as can be and lights up any room she's in with her adorable smile. She is great with people and other dogs, and is also great with kids. She loves treats and loves to play outside but always makes time for her naps. She is sure to bring love to your home. Say hello to the adorable Bailey!

Special Considerations

Since Bailey is on the verge of her senior years (5 years and older for large dogs), it is important to remember that she may develop health problems due to old age.

If you are interested in adopting Bailey, please call us at 111-222-3333 or email us at bestfriends@email.com. You can also drop by our shelter located at 123 Main Street in Anytown, Texas. The adoption fee is $50.
