Meet Bella!

Bella is a 5 year old female Staffordshire Terrier who loves to play! She is energetic, playful, and, if you would let her, she would play outside for most of the day. Some of her favorite activities include swimming and playing fetch. She gets along well with other dogs and loves to be around people. Even though, she is hyper, she is still gentle around small children, making her the perfect family dog. Say hello to the lovable Bella!

Special Considerations

Bella is a young, energetic dog, so she needs time set aside everyday for exercise. This can be something as simple as taking a walk, playing fetch, or going to the dog park. If an active dog like Bella doesn't get enough exercise and activity, some dogs like her might resort to damaging things around the house. Bella would best be suited for an active family that has enough time to exercise her.

If you are interested in adopting Bella, please call us at 111-222-3333 or email us at bestfriends@email.com. You can also drop by our shelter located at 123 Main Street in Anytown, Texas. The adoption fee is $50.
