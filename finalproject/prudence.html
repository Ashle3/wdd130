Meet Prudence!

Prudence, also known as Pru, is a 7-year-old female English Bulldog. Most volunteers here call her the 'Little Old Lady' because sometimes, it seems like she is an 80-year-old woman trapped inside a bulldog body. She is laid-back, loves to cuddle, and is sure to let you know when dinner is late. She gets along well with people but is hesitant towards other dogs. Her ideal household would be one without dogs and small children. We love the Little Old Lady here at Best Friends, and we're sure you will too. Say hello to the fabulous Pru!

Special Considerations

English Bulldogs generally have a lot of health problems so it is best if the owner is patient and is able to attend to Pru's health needs. It is also best practice to thoroughly clean between her wrinkles as failing to do so can lead to infection.

If you are interested in adopting Prudence, please call us at 111-222-3333 or email us at bestfriends@email.com. You can also drop by our shelter located at 123 Main Street in Anytown, Texas. The adoption fee is $50.
