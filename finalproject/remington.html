Meet Remington!

Remington is a 9-year-old male Golden Retriever that is lovable and laid back. He loves treats, toys, and loves to be outside even if it is just to sleep all day. He gets along well with other dogs and is great with small children. He does have diabetes, so special care does need to be taken regarding his health. Remington is a gentle giant and we hope to find him a home soon. Say hello to the gentle giant, Remington!

Special Considerations

Remington does have diabetes, so constant care is needed, and vet bills might be expensive. It is best if Remington goes to an owner that is patient and is willing to attend to his medical needs.

If you are interested in adopting Remington, please call us at 111-222-3333 or email us at bestfriends@email.com. You can also drop by our shelter located at 123 Main Street in Anytown, Texas. The adoption fee is $50.
