Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a rafting expedition last?

Typically, our rafting expeditions can last anywhere from an hour to an hour and a half, depending on the conditions of the river.

Is it safe for my kids to participate in the expeditions?

Yes, it is safe for kids to participate in our non-whitewater rafting expeditions! However, we recommend that kids be at least 10 years of age to participate in our whitewater expedition, as some parts may be too intense for young children.

Can I bring my pet on the rafting expedition?

No, pets are not allowed on any of our rafting expeditions. However, there are many hiking trails near the river that are perfect for taking your pet on a walk!

Will I get a full refund if I cancel?

You will receive a full refund if you cancel before the 48-hour mark before your visit. However, if you cancel within 48 hours before your visit, you will receive a $25 dollar fee along with your full refund. If you cancel within 24 before your visit, you will be charged a $50 fee along with your full refund.

Are we allowed to take pictures during the expedition?

Yes, you are allowed to take pictures during the expedition, but do so at your own risk. Dry Oar is not responsible for any items that are damaged or lost during the expedition.
