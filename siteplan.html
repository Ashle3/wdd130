Site Name

Dry Oar Rafting Company

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help users learn about our company and its standards, learn what to expect when going on one of our rafting expeditions (including overall experience, price, payment options, etc.), and easily schedule appointments for a rafting expedition. Our goal is for the site to be user friendly and easy to navigate.

Site Audience

  • Is there a rafting expedition that's within my budget?
  • When is the next available rafting expedition?
  • What is the max number of people I can book for an expedition?
  • What is the payment policy? Will I get a refund if I cancel within a certain number of days?

Site Logo

Color Scheme

  • I am going to use "Kombu Green" as a background color for areas that don't have regular text. HEX: #283618, RGB: 40, 54, 24
  • I am going to use the color "Liver Dogs" as as an accent color, especially for the Kombu Green. HEX: #BC6C25, RGB: 188, 108, 37
  • I am going to use "White" as a background color for the regular text, to make the text readable. HEX: #FFFFFF, RGB: 255, 255, 255

Site Typography

The font I am going to use for my heading is "Architect's Daughter". The font I am going to use for my regular text is "Open Sans".
